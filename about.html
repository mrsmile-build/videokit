Built to help you create better content faster

VideoKit is a free AI-powered tool that turns your ideas into complete faceless videos — script, stock footage, voiceover — in minutes.

What is VideoKit?

VideoKit was built for content creators, entrepreneurs, and businesses who want to produce professional video content without cameras, studios, or expensive software.

You type your idea. AI writes the script, finds matching stock videos, generates a voiceover, and gives you everything ready to post on Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, or Facebook.

It's completely free to use — no subscription, no credit card.

What you can do with it

✍️

AI Script Writing

Generate full video scripts with hook, scenes, and CTA in seconds. Customize with the built-in chat assistant.

🎬

Stock Video Search

Automatically finds matching stock footage for each scene from thousands of free Pixabay videos.

🎙️

AI Voiceover

Generate narration in multiple voices — American, British, Australian, French, German, Spanish and more.

📝

Auto Captions

Text is automatically burned onto your video clips so your content works even without sound.

⬇️

Download Everything

Download your script, audio, and video clips separately or combined into one MP4 file.

🔄

Script Customizer

Use the chatbox to tell AI exactly how to change your script — scene by scene.
