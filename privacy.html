Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how VideoKit ("we", "us", or "our"), operated by MrSmile Build, collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website at mrsmile-build.github.io/videokit.

1. Information We Collect

VideoKit is designed to be privacy-friendly. We collect minimal information:

  • Video ideas and scripts: Text you enter is sent to our AI service (Groq) to generate scripts. We do not store this data permanently.
  • Usage data: We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics (pages visited, time on site, general location). This does not identify you personally.
  • Contact form submissions: If you contact us, we collect your name, email address, and message to respond to your inquiry.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate AI scripts and video content as requested
  • To improve VideoKit based on anonymous usage patterns
  • To respond to your support requests and feedback

3. Third-Party Services

VideoKit uses the following third-party services:

  • Groq AI — Script generation. Your input text is processed by Groq's API.
  • Pixabay — Stock video search. Search queries are sent to Pixabay's API.
  • Google Analytics — Anonymous usage tracking.
  • Render.com — Backend API hosting.

Each service has its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing them if you have concerns.

4. Data Storage

VideoKit does not maintain a database of user data. Your scripts, selected videos, and generated content exist only in your browser session and are deleted when you close the tab.

5. Cookies

We use Google Analytics cookies for anonymous usage tracking. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

6. Children's Privacy

VideoKit is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request information about any data we hold about you, or to request its deletion. Contact us at mrsmile4569@gmail.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact Us
